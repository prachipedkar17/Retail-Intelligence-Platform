Retail Intelligence Platform: End-to-End Sales \& Customer Analytics



Project Overview



This project is an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution developed using Power BI, SQL, and Python. It analyzes retail sales, customer behavior, product performance, and regional trends through interactive dashboards, helping businesses make data-driven decisions.



Objectives



\- Analyze overall sales and profit.

\- Understand customer purchasing behavior.

\- Identify top-performing products and categories.

\- Compare sales and profit across regions.

\- Provide an executive-level business summary.
